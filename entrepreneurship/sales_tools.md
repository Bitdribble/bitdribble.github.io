#### LinkedIn automation and outreach

Third-party review snapshots (verify current scores): Dux-Soup often lands near mid–high fours on G2, Capterra, and Trustpilot; praise centers on time saved and support, complaints on UI age, browser load, and occasional billing friction.

**Platform risk:** LinkedIn states it does not allow third-party software, plug-ins, or extensions that scrape or automate activity on LinkedIn; account restriction is possible regardless of vendor.

| Tool | ≤20 word summary |
|------|------------------|
| [Dux-Soup](https://www.dux-soup.com) | Browser and cloud LinkedIn automation; entry near $15/mo; reviews cite time saved, dated UI, policy risk. |
| [Dripify](https://dripify.io) | Cloud-first LinkedIn plus email automation; about $59/mo basic; large G2 footprint; cleaner setup than budget extensions. |
| [Expandi](https://expandi.io) | Premium LinkedIn and email smart sequences; about $99/mo; strong support and ease-of-use feedback on G2. |
| [Waalaxy](https://www.waalaxy.com) | LinkedIn automation plus email on higher tiers; huge G2 volume; entry near €19/user/mo with step-up pricing. |
| [Meet Alfred](https://meetalfred.com) | Multi-channel LinkedIn outreach automation aimed at individuals and small teams. |
| [Linked Helper](https://www.linkedhelper.com) | Desktop LinkedIn automation; cheap Standard and Pro tiers; deeper discounts on longer commitments. |
| [SalesRobot](https://salesrobot.ai) | Multichannel LinkedIn and email with AI personalization; heavier AI story than basic extension tools. |
| [HeyReach](https://www.heyreach.io) | Sender-based pricing for agencies managing many LinkedIn accounts; poor fit for solo low-volume use. |
| [PhantomBuster](https://phantombuster.com) | General automation and scraping platform; LinkedIn is one workflow among many; from about $59/mo. |

##### Open source and self-hosted

| Tool | ≤20 word summary |
|------|------------------|
| [OpenOutreach](https://openoutreach.app) | Self-hosted open-source LinkedIn automation for B2B prospecting; niche versus mature commercial sequencers. |
| [Mautic](https://www.mautic.org) | Open-source marketing automation for campaigns and email; stack component, not a LinkedIn-only product. |
| [listmonk](https://listmonk.app) | Self-hosted open-source newsletter and list manager; pairs with Mautic or n8n for outbound email. |
| [n8n](https://n8n.io) | Self-hostable workflow automation; native LinkedIn node centers on posting; advanced flows use HTTP or browser helpers. |

Smaller GitHub or extension experiments (e.g. linkedin-autoconnect-style projects) are not drop-in substitutes for the products above.

##### Side-by-side (indicative; confirm on vendor sites)

| Tool | Lowest public monthly (typical entry) | Core motion | Email / multichannel | Best fit |
|------|----------------------------------------|-------------|----------------------|----------|
| Dux-Soup | ~$14.99/mo Pro; cloud tiers higher | LinkedIn automation | Main plans LinkedIn-first; cloud adds managed execution | Budget solo reps wanting LinkedIn actions |
| Dripify | ~$59/mo Basic | Cloud LinkedIn automation | Yes — LinkedIn plus email, runs in cloud | Cloud-first LinkedIn + email |
| Expandi | ~$99/mo | LinkedIn automation | Yes — LinkedIn and email sequences | Premium all-in-one outreach |
| Waalaxy | ~€19/user/mo Pro | LinkedIn automation | Business tier adds LinkedIn + email campaigns | Ladder from cheap LinkedIn-only to multichannel |
| Linked Helper | ~$15/mo Standard | LinkedIn automation | Desktop app; limited email emphasis in pricing pages | Price-sensitive power users |
| HeyReach | ~$79/mo one sender | LinkedIn at scale | Inbox and credits; sender-based, not pure email sequencer | Agencies, many accounts |
| PhantomBuster | ~$59/mo | Automation / scraping | Platform workflows, not a single-purpose sequencer | Broader automation beyond LinkedIn messaging |

##### Practical shortlist

* **Solo, tight budget:** Dux-Soup or Linked Helper first.
* **Cloud LinkedIn + email:** Dripify, Expandi, or Waalaxy Business.
* **Agency scale:** HeyReach.
* **Broader automation toolkit:** PhantomBuster.
* **OSS / self-hosted direct:** OpenOutreach; **stack:** Mautic + listmonk + n8n (LinkedIn activity still carries policy risk).
