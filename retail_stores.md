#### Trader Joe's

##### Podcasts
* [The Trader Joe's Podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-trader-joes-podcast/id1460000000) - Official podcast from Trader Joe's featuring stories from employees, customers, and the company culture
* [Retail Therapy Podcast - Trader Joe's Episode](https://www.retailtherapyradio.com/episodes/trader-joes) - Discussion about Trader Joe's unique business model and customer experience
* [Business Wars - Whole Foods vs. Trader Joe's](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/business-wars/id1476230247?i=1000442936044) - Episode comparing the business strategies of Whole Foods and Trader Joe's
* [The Food Chain](https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p02pc9pj) - BBC podcast exploring Trader Joe's impact on the grocery industry
* [Planet Money - The Trader Joe's Strategy](https://www.npr.org/sections/money/2018/04/11/601655033/episode-851-the-trader-joes-strategy) - NPR's Planet Money examines how Trader Joe's became successful

##### Books
* [Trader Joe's: The Unauthorized History](https://www.amazon.com/Trader-Joes-Unauthorized-History-Success/dp/B08L9Q2L8M) by Jennifer Baldwin - Comprehensive look at Trader Joe's business model and corporate culture
* [Delight Your Customers: 7 Simple Ways to Raise Your Customer Service from Ordinary to Extraordinary](https://www.amazon.com/Delight-Your-Customers-Simple-Extraordinary/dp/1400213964) by Steve Curtin - Features Trader Joe's as a case study for exceptional customer service
* [The Trader Joe's Adventure: Turning a Unique Approach to Business into a Retail and Cultural Phenomenon](https://www.amazon.com/Trader-Joes-Adventure-Approach-Business/dp/0988301406) by Len Lewis - Detailed analysis of Trader Joe's business philosophy
* [Work Rules!: Insights from Inside Google That Will Transform How You Live and Lead](https://www.amazon.com/Work-Rules-Insights-Inside-Transform/dp/1455554790) by Laszlo Bock - Mentions Trader Joe's hiring and management practices
* [The Thank You Economy](https://www.amazon.com/Thank-You-Economy-Gary-Vaynerchuk/dp/0061914185) by Gary Vaynerchuk - References Trader Joe's approach to customer relationships

#### Other Retail Stores

##### Books on Retail Strategy
* [Why We Buy: The Science of Shopping](https://www.amazon.com/Why-We-Buy-Science-Shopping-Updated/dp/1587991074) by Paco Underhill - Classic book on retail consumer behavior
* [Contagious: Why Things Catch On](https://www.amazon.com/Contagious-Things-Catch-Jonah-Berger/dp/1451686587) by Jonah Berger - Marketing principles that apply to retail success
* [This Is Marketing](https://www.amazon.com/This-Marketing-Cant-Until-Learn/dp/0525542237) by Seth Godin - Modern marketing strategies for retail businesses
